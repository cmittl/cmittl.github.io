RESILIENT FOODS


Resilient Foods is a company aiming to solve food insecurity while also creating personal connections in the community. Specifically, we follow a model in which part of all profits from each purchase will donated to a food-bank or similar organization or directly to a family in need.


Project Roadmap

  1. Initial Research
    • Looking to Website platforms and layouts
    • Understanding Google AdWorks
    • Figuring out integration with Google Analytics and payments
    • Researching similar business models
    • Creating and disseminating Qualtrics survey
    • Certification with WIC and SNAP programs
  2. Website Design
    • Color Scheme
    • Logo
    • Required pages
    • User flow
    • Website Mockups
  3. Website Coding
    • Creating basic functionality of primary-purchaser side
    • Implementing front-end of each of the pages
    • Using payments API to allow for credit card purchases
    • Integrating with Google Analytics
  4. User Analytics
    • Tracking where users are coming from
    • Understanding what products are doing the best
    • Figuring out if the business model is working
    • Making changes based on customer usage and feedback
